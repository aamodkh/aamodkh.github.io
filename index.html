About me

I am Aamod Khatiwada, a PhD in Computer Science student at Khoury College of Computer Sciences, Northeastern University, Boston, Massachusetts, USA. I am originally from Jhorahat, a town in the eastern Terai of Nepal. Although I am yet to dig in and select a proper direction, my research is primarily related to the database and its different aspects.

During free times, I enjoy watching cricket and soccer, listening to the music, roaming around the streets, and writing poems, stories, and news articles. I am also interested in the South Asian politics and follow it closely. 😉

About me

I am Aamod Khatiwada, a PhD Candidate (Computer Science, Databases) at Khoury College of Computer Sciences, Northeastern University, Boston, MA, USA. I am originally from Jhorahat, a town in the southeastern part of Nepal. My primary research focus is to develop novel techniques and algorithms that find and integrate open data tables in a principled way.

During free times, I enjoy watching cricket and soccer, writing poems, stories, and news articles.

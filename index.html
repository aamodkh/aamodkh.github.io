About

Dr. Lin Chen is an assistant professor at Department of Computer Science, Texas Tech University. Before joining Texas Tech, he was a postdoc at Technical University of Berlin, Technical University of Munich, and then a research assistant professor at University of Houston. His research interests lie generally in the field of algorithms and complexity, distributed systems and cybersecurity. His research work has been published at top-tier conferences including SODA, SPAA, AAAI, IJCAI, and top-tier journals including SIAM journal on computing, ACM transactions on algorithms, etc. His research is sponsored by NSF and DHS. He is currently the sole PI of NSF 1756014
